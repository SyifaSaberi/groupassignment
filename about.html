NASSS Boutique






NASSS Boutique is the name we chose for our company. NASSS Boutique gets its name from the first letter of each member of our business group. We sell apparel such as blouses and hijabs. We believe that the name of this boutique will entice customers to shop at our store. This business is run with a 5 persons partnership. They are Nur Aliah Izyany, Syifa Nadzirah, Nurul Syazwani, Natasya Nabila and Nur Shuhada. Our main activity is manufacturing. We make several types of blouses and scarves. Our main product is blouse. The premise is No 5 second floor, Bangunan Lagenda Height, 08500 Sungai Petani, Kedah. The chosen location can be considered strategic since it is near Sungai Petani town. The start date is 1st December 2022. We chose the start date at the beginning of the month to facilitate expenses and so on.
